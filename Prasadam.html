Prasadam

aham vaishvanaro bhutva praninam deham ashritahpranapana-samayuktah pachamy annam chatur-vidham

BG 15.14: It is I who take the form of the fire of digestion in the stomachs of all living beings, and combine with the incoming and outgoing breaths, to digest and assimilate the four kinds of foods.

In essence it says that the food that is offered to the Lord or consumed must be pure and must be sattvik in nature. Also food is something that brings people together. Sri Shankara Peetham is pleased to offer the devotees food prepared at the Peetham for the private religious events, a pooja at home or even for your special occasions.   

We provide traditionally cooked Hindu Religious Authentic food for various occasions and religious events.
